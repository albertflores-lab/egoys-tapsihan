Our Story

The Story Behind Egoy's

What started as a small turo-turo in Quezon City has grown into one of the most beloved tapsilogan in the area. Founded by Aldrin "Egoy" Perez, we serve over 500 orders daily.

We started with just three silog meals and a dream to feed the neighborhood at any hour. Now — 24 hours, 7 days a week — we're still here.

Who We Are

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To serve authentic, affordable Filipino comfort food to every Filipino — students, workers, and families — 24 hours a day, with the warmth of a home-cooked meal.

Our Vision

To become the go-to tapsilogan brand in Metro Manila, known for consistent quality, speed, and a Filipino dining experience that never sleeps.

Our Values

Authenticity in every recipe. Respect for every customer. Commitment to freshness. Pride in being Filipino. And a smile — even at 3AM.

Our Community

We serve students, jeepney drivers, office workers, night owls, and families. Everyone is welcome at Egoy's table — no matter the hour.

Timeline

Our Journey

2019
Aldrin Perez opens Egoy's as a small street stall in Quezon City with 3 menu items and ₱5,000 capital.
2020
Survived the pandemic by pivoting to delivery. Introduced the beloved Unlimited Lugaw during lockdown.
2021
Expanded to a proper dine-in spot with 10 tables. First 24/7 operations began.
2023
Launched online ordering via Facebook and Messenger. Reached 300 daily orders milestone.
2025
Launched this PWA for seamless online ordering, GCash/Maya payments, and delivery tracking.
